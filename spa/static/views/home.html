The project

Our project is to be able to get recommendations, not based on the popularity of TV shows, but on the actual content of seasons and episodes. This way, you can get objective advices on what to watch, and discover new TV shows depending on what you like.

video_library

The NLP engine

Our cutting edge algorithms of recommendation are based on Natural Language Processing, allowing us to extract meaningful keywords through a semantic analysis of synopsis. This NLP engine relies on the Python libraries NLTK and WordNet.
